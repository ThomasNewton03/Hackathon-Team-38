Stress to Impress

Welcome to Stress to Impress!

You're playing as a character who has just started at a new school and wants to make a good first impression. Your job is to make sure they're Coolness Meter remains as full as possible so that your character doesn't become an outcast.

You will play for 5 days, each day will have a different scenario you need to dress for. Be careful what you pick, as this will influence your character's day at school!
